- OpenMap constructs an open-vocabulary visual-language map. 
- It performs fine-grained, instance-level semantic mapping on navigation scenes from Matterport3D, and accurately grounds generic instructions to the targets, where darker regions in the heatmaps indicate stronger alignment between the instruction and the predicted instance.
